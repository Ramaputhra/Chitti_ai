# CHITTI Companion: End-User Features

Below is a summary of the core capabilities and features that CHITTI provides to you as an end-user, based on the cognitive architecture developed up to Phase L.

## 🧠 Advanced Memory & Knowledge
*   **Infinite Personal Memory (Episodic Memory):** CHITTI remembers your past conversations, decisions, and activities. You never have to repeat yourself or remind CHITTI of what you discussed last week.
*   **Deep Concept Understanding (Semantic Graph):** CHITTI doesn't just store text; it builds a connected "web" of knowledge about your projects, preferences, and workflows. It understands how different concepts relate to each other.
*   **Smart Knowledge Lifetimes:** CHITTI intrinsically understands that some information is permanent (like architectural rules) while other information is temporary (like today's weather or a quick scratchpad note).

## ⚖️ Intelligent Reasoning & Trust
*   **Automated Conflict Detection:** If a new document contradicts an old note, or if you change your mind about a project requirement, CHITTI detects the disagreement in the background instead of getting confused or hallucinating.
*   **Explainable Answers:** Whenever CHITTI gives you an answer or makes a decision, it can tell you *exactly* why, providing a breakdown of its confidence, the source's authority, and the historical consensus.
*   **Source Authority & Trust:** CHITTI knows the difference between an official specification and a casual brainstorming note. It naturally trusts highly authoritative sources more, ensuring you get accurate, high-quality answers.

## 🔄 Seamless Context & Continuity
*   **Always-On Context Awareness:** CHITTI continuously tracks your active workspace, desktop state, and current goals. It knows what you are working on before you even type a message.
*   **Graceful Context Switching:** If you are deep into coding and suddenly ask a question about your calendar, CHITTI can instantly pivot, answer your question, and smoothly restore your coding context without losing its train of thought.

## ⚙️ Robust Execution (Foundation)
*   **Observable Task Management:** When CHITTI performs long-running tasks, it does so in an observable, auditable way. You can always see what it is thinking, planning, and executing.
*   **Deterministic Safety:** CHITTI operates under a strict set of constitutional rules. It will never synthesize fake knowledge, permanently delete your authoritative records, or take dangerous actions without explicit permission.
