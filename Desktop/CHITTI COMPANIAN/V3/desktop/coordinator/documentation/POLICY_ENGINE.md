# CHITTI V2 — POLICY ENGINE SPECIFICATION

Supported Orchestration Policies:
- `Minimal UI`: Hides background widgets, minimal character presence.
- `Presentation Mode`: Prioritizes slide deck and laser pointer.
- `Gaming Mode`: Suppresses toast notifications and auto-docks character.
- `Focus Mode`: Displays productivity widget only.
- `Silent Mode`: Suppresses voice animations and audio indicators.
- `Accessibility Mode`: High contrast and enlarged widget anchors.
- `Battery Saver`: Lowers frame targets for background indicators.
- `Developer Mode`: Full debug overlay and timeline inspection active.
