# Sprint 14: Attention & World State

## 1. Goal
Complete Milestone F (Attention & World State). Build the cognitive gateway that prevents the Planner from being overwhelmed by raw multimodal data. Introduce the Perception Bus, Attention Runtime, and Unified World State.

## 2. Deliverables
- `Priority` and `AttentionEvent` models.
- `PerceptionBus` to normalize raw signals.
- `SalienceCalculator` and `AttentionEngine`.
- `FocusManager` and `AttentionPolicyManager`.
- `WorldState` model (including Execution and Temporal Contexts).
- Planner Tick integration.
